# **App Name**: Viloryi

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog Display: Showcase curated fashion collections by categories like Kurti Sets, Kurtis, and Co-ord Sets, along with featured products presented in an interactive grid with hover effects.
- Product Detail Viewer: Provide dedicated product pages with high-resolution images, detailed descriptions, sizing options, and an 'Add to Cart' functionality.
- Shopping Cart Functionality: Enable users to add, view, update quantities, and remove items from their shopping cart before proceeding to checkout.
- Streamlined Guest Checkout: Facilitate a secure and intuitive guest checkout process, allowing customers to complete purchases by providing shipping and payment details without requiring account creation.
- Dynamic Homepage Sections: Present a compelling hero section ('Wear Your Elegance'), organized category links, 'Why Choose Us' content, and a placeholder for customer testimonials/stories, aligning with brand identity.
- Mobile-First Responsive Design: Ensure the website delivers an optimal viewing and interaction experience across all devices, prioritizing mobile accessibility and performance with fluid layouts and interactions.
- AI Style Curator tool: An AI-powered tool that suggests personalized clothing combinations or individual pieces to users based on their style preferences or a selected occasion, enhancing discovery.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated, deep, warm charcoal (#39332E) representing luxury and grounding.
- Background color: A very light, soft beige (#F8F5F3) for an expansive, clean, and elegant canvas.
- Accent color: A muted, dusty rose (#BD9597) to provide a touch of warmth and feminine elegance against the primary and background tones.
- Headlines: 'Alegreya' (humanist serif) for an elegant, intellectual, and contemporary feel. Body text: 'Inter' (grotesque sans-serif) for a modern, clean, and highly readable look.
- Minimalist, line-art icons that maintain a clean and uncluttered premium aesthetic, reflecting simplicity and luxury.
- Emphasis on generous whitespace, clean lines, and a structured modular grid system to create a premium, unburdened, and easy-to-navigate interface, with a mobile-first approach.
- Subtle and smooth transitions for all interactive elements, such as product hover effects and navigation changes, to contribute to a fluid and premium user experience.
